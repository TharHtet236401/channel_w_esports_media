Wai Yan Linn, known professionally as Wai Wai, is a Burmese caster and commentator with years of experience in the Mobile Legends: Bang Bang esports scene.

He has cast at major international events including the M7 World Championship (2026), M6 World Championship (2024), M4 World Championship (2023), and M1 World Championship (2019), as well as the MLBB Mid Season Cups and Snapdragon Pro Series APAC Finals.

Channel W was founded to bring his passion for esports and regional coverage to fans across Southeast Asia and beyond—covering news, tournaments, and the people behind the scenes.
